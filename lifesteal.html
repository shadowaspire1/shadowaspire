About Lifesteal

In Lifesteal mode, when you kill a player you steal one of their hearts ❤️. If you lose all hearts you can be banned from the server. Fight players, build bases, and survive.

Lifesteal Rules

  • No hacks or cheats (X-ray, fly, kill aura, exploits).
  • No spawn killing or camping respawn.
  • No alt killing or farming hearts.
  • No unfair teaming outside clans.
  • Respect other players and avoid toxic behavior.
  • Staff decisions are final.
